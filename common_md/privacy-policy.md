# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you visit this blog.

## 1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal identification information (PII) through this site. We do not use cookies or trackers to follow your browsing habits.

## 2. Information Usage

Since we do not collect data, we do not have anything to use, sell, or share with third parties.

## 3. Third-Party Links

Our blog contains links to other websites (e.g., GitHub, Next.js). Once you leave our site, our privacy policy no longer applies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of those third-party sites.

## 4. Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact the site owner.
